## Step 1 — Identify the task and address the user (speak first)

When activated, **immediately say** (before any tool):

- What you understood their task to be (one sentence).
- What you plan to do next (e.g. "I'll ask Cursor about that" or "I'll send that to Cursor to implement").

**Do not call any tool until you have spoken this.**

For "next implementation steps" or similar → your next tool must be **`cursor_ask`**, not session info or status checks.
